The application needs permission to talk to your Spotify account. This allows it to see which song plays.

1. Launch the Forza Horizon Spotify Radio application from your Start menu.
2. Sign in with your Spotify account details.
3. A browser window will open. Enter your login data there.
4. Accept the permissions request.
5. The application confirms the connection status on the main dashboard.

## 🏎️ Running the Integration

Syncing music to your drive takes little effort. Use these steps every time you want to play:

1. Start your Forza Horizon game as you normally do.
2. Run the Forza Horizon Spotify Radio application.
3. Open your Spotify desktop player and ensure your music plays there.
4. The application detects the game window.
5. It mutes the in-game music radio.
6. Your Spotify tracks play over the game audio engine.
7. Use the interface to skip tracks or change playlists without leaving the game screen.

This tool reads telemetry data from the game. It does not modify game files or cheat. Use it for music playback only.

**Does it work with Forza Horizon 4, 5, and 6?**
Yes. The app detects the specific Forza game currently running on your system. It applies the correct settings for each version.

**Can I choose which playlist plays?**
Yes. Use the app dashboard to select any playlist from your Spotify library. The app follows your selection.

**Is it safe?**
The software processes data locally on your computer. It does not store your account password. All authentication happens through the official Spotify portal.
